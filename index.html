About Me

Hi, my name is Christian! I started my journey in tech with a focus on Software Engineering, where I developed coding skills and participated in hackathons that challenged me to think creatively and solve problems. While I enjoyed software development, I discovered a passion for troubleshooting and problem-solving that led me to transition into the IT field.

In IT, I’ve worked as a Help Desk Support Technician, where I gained hands-on experience resolving technical issues and delivering excellent customer service. Along the way, I earned certifications like the CompTIA A+ and Google IT Certificate, solidifying my technical foundation and deepening my expertise in hardware and software troubleshooting.

Now, I’m on a path to specialize in cybersecurity, working to combine my background in software and IT with advanced skills in cyber defense. I’m currently studying for the Security+ certification, pursuing a Bachelor's in Cybersecurity at Western Governors University, and participating in the Hack The Box SOC program to gain practical experience as a Security Operations Center (SOC) analyst.

I built this website as a fun and creative way to showcase my certifications, degree, projects, and contact information. It reflects my journey so far and the exciting challenges ahead in the cybersecurity field!
