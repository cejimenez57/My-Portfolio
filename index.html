About Me

Hi, I’m Christian! My journey in IT began in college, where I developed coding skills and participated in hackathons that challenged me to think creatively and solve problems. Over time, I expanded my expertise to include troubleshooting both hardware and software, earning my CompTIA A+ certification and Google IT Certificate to solidify my technical foundation. I’ve also worked as a Help Desk Support Technician, where I honed my customer service skills and deepened my technical knowledge by resolving a wide range of user issues.

Now, I’m diving into the world of cybersecurity. I’m studying for the Security+ certification, working toward a Bachelor’s in Cybersecurity at Western Governors University, and participating in the self-paced Hack The Box SOC program, where I’m learning about cyber defense and how to think like a Security Operations Center (SOC) analyst.

I’m passionate about continuous learning and bringing a well-rounded technical approach to solving complex challenges in the IT and cybersecurity fields.
